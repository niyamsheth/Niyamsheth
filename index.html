04 — Process

How ideas become
visual reality.

01

Research

Deep dive into the brand, audience, competitors, and cultural context to uncover the real story.

02

Ideation

Rapid sketching, moodboarding, and concept exploration — generating 50+ directions before narrowing down.

03

Wireframing

Structural layouts and composition studies. Grid systems, hierarchy, and spatial relationships take shape.

04

Design

High-fidelity execution with obsessive attention to typography, color, texture, and visual rhythm.

05

Refinement

Pixel-perfect polish, feedback integration, and multiple revision rounds until every detail is right.

06

Delivery

Production-ready files, brand guidelines, and comprehensive handoff with all assets organized.
